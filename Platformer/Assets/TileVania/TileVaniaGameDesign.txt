TileVania Game Design:
1.Player Experience:
Under pressure.
2.Core Mechanic:
Run and jump.
3.Game Theme:
Escape. You're a prisoner. Clock is ticking.
4.Core Game Loop:
Get from A to B by navigating platforms and avoiding traps and enemies.

MVP(minimal viable product) gameplay features:
1.Character movement: player can run and jump.
2.Traps / obstacles.
3.Start and end points; mechanism to complete the level.
4.Countdown timer: if reaches zerp, game over.
